Overview
About the Conference

ICAISTLAM 2027 is a premier interdisciplinary platform bringing together academicians, researchers, scientists, industry professionals, and students to present and discuss the latest advancements in Artificial Intelligence and its applications across Applied Sciences, Mathematics, Engineering, and Education.

The conference fosters collaboration between academia, research institutions, and industry through keynote lectures, technical sessions, and scholarly discussions. It aspires to contribute significantly to scientific advancement, technological innovation, and knowledge dissemination.

Organised by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Tiruchirappalli.

All accepted and registered papers will be published in the AIP conference proceedings (approval pending), subjected to double-blind peer review.

Institution
About SRM Institute of Science and Technology

SRM Institute of Science and Technology is one of India's leading multidisciplinary higher education institutions, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, innovation, and industry engagement. Established with a vision to impart quality education and foster technological advancement, SRMIST offers comprehensive programmes across Engineering, Management, Medicine, Science, Law, Agricultural Sciences, and Allied Health Sciences.

In NIRF 2025, SRMIST achieved the 22nd rank Overall and 11th among universities in India, reflecting its commitment to quality higher education and research excellence.

With campuses in Kattankulathur (Chennai), Ramapuram, Vadapalani, Tiruchirappalli, Modinagar, Sonepat, Amaravati, and Gangtok, SRMIST has established itself as a nationally recognized centre of excellence. The university attracts students from across India and many countries, creating a vibrant multicultural environment.

Department
Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of CSE at SRMIST Tiruchirappalli was established in 2021 with the vision of imparting quality education and fostering innovation in computing. It promotes research in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, and the Internet of Things.

The department collaborates with leading organizations through internships, workshops, expert talks, and industry-driven projects, bridging academia and industry effectively.

Vision

To provide quality education in Computer Science and Engineering and produce highly competent engineers who can serve society on a global level.

Mission

To provide industry-oriented training that prepares graduates for real-world challenges, while emphasising ethical conduct and social responsibility.

Conference Scope
Objective of the Conference

ICAISTLAM 2027 aims to provide a premier interdisciplinary platform for academicians, researchers, scientists, industry professionals, and students to present and discuss the latest advancements in Artificial Intelligence and its applications across Applied Sciences, Mathematics, Engineering, Education, and emerging technologies.

The conference seeks to highlight the transformative role of AI in addressing complex scientific, mathematical, engineering, and societal challenges while encouraging the development of intelligent and sustainable solutions. It facilitates interdisciplinary research, identifies emerging trends, and provides networking opportunities among researchers and practitioners.

The conference focuses on AI-driven solutions that enhance educational practices while accelerating scientific discovery. It serves as a premier forum for presenting cutting-edge research, innovative methodologies, and practical AI applications, fostering collaboration among academia, industry, and research organizations.

International Advisory Board: SRM's IAB, established in 2003, consists of 50+ distinguished deans, directors, and professors from Harvard Business School, MIT, Stanford, LSE, UC Berkeley, Cambridge, NUS, and many more of the world's leading institutions.

Review Process
  1. Scope Check

    All manuscripts are first evaluated for alignment with the conference scope and themes.

  2. Plagiarism Screening

    Screened submissions undergo quality check via Turnitin. Only papers with similarity index below 5% proceed.

  3. Single-Blind Review

    Qualifying papers are reviewed by three independent referees through a single-blind peer review process.

  4. Consensus Decision

    A consensus of all three reviewers is mandatory for acceptance. Each reviewer handles at most three manuscripts.

Best Paper Award

One Best Paper will be selected from each track based on the quality and impact of the research. Winners will be announced at the Valedictory Function on 16th April 2027.
